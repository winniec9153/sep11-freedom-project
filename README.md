## Context
I am a student at [HSTAT](https://www.hstat.org/) in the [Software Engineering Program](https://hstatsep.github.io/). The "Freedom Project" for SEP11 is a year-long project all about making something using JavaScript along with a third-party JS tool.

For my project, I chose to independently study p5Play in order to help me make my Budgeting App. So p5Play is a JavaScript library that adds sprites, animations, and game mechanics to p5.js, making it easier to create 2D games. I use p5Play for the background animation of my app which made it less boring. I choose my topic of budgeting because now a days teenagers and adult don't keep track of their finances abd spent their money recklessly. Through this budgeting app they will be more financially organized and manage their money more wisely.

## Implications
##### PROS
* A pro of this app is that it keeps track of your spending and income over time. Whenever you make a purchase or earn money, you can log it into the app, and the transaction will be recorded in the daily log. This allows users to easily monitor their finances, review their spending habits, and stay organized.
* Another pro is that ot is simple and convenient to use, reducing the need for manual calculations or spreadsheets. So the users can quickly enter their income and expenses, and the app will automatically organizes and calculates their financial information that they entered. This will help saves time, reduces errors, and makes it easier for users to understand their spending habits and stay within their budget.
##### CONS
* A con of this budgeting app is that the daily log is not actually a permanent daily log. Users cannot go back and view their previous financial transactions, spending, or earnings because the log resets each time the app is restarted.
* Another con of this budgeting app is that it does not handle negative numbers correctly. If the balance is already negative and the user enters a transaction with a negative value, the calculation may become inaccurate. As a result, the app may not work properly when negative numbers are entered in both input fields.
